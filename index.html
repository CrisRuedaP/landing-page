About Us


We are a strong and diverse team made up of 3 software developers who believe deeply that creativity, innovation, and resilience are the most powerful tools to change society for the better.

One of those days in our journey through Holberton School, concerned about our future as junior developers looking for a job in this chaotic world during the COVID-19 pandemic, we imagined Jolby as an alternative for us and for our peers around the world to find job opportunities but also as a way to innovate and use the power that tech gave us, to adapt ourselves to this new world that has changed forever in a way or another.

As a team, we have given our best to bring this project to reality, and for us, would be hugely rewarding if this tool at least improves your experience looking for a job, the way it is now improving ours.
